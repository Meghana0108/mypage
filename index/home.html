In palmistry, marriage lines (also known as relationship lines or union lines) are the small horizontal lines located on the side of the palm, just below the base of the little finger (on the Mercury mount). These lines are believed to indicate aspects of a person's relationships, including marriage, long-term partnerships, and sometimes significant romantic relationships. - *Intersection with other lines*: If a marriage line intersects with other lines on the palm, it might symbolize the influence of external factors on the relationship, such as career, health, or children. - *Presence of islands or chains*: Islands or chains on the marriage line might represent periods of turmoil, misunderstandings, or stress within the relationship. It's important to note that palmistry is considered a pseudoscience, and interpretations of palm lines, including marriage lines, are not based on scientific evidence. Interpretations can vary greatly depending on cultural beliefs and the individual palm reader.
